A ball is thrown vertically upward. At its maximum height, what can be said about its velocity and acceleration?

check

Correct! Well done.

Gravity is a constant force near Earth's surface. Even when the object momentarily stops moving at its peak, the pull of gravity continues to act on it at 9.8 m/s².
