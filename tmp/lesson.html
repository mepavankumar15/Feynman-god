Gravity's Arc: Projectile Motion

Understanding how objects move through the air under the sole influence of gravity. It's the mathematics behind every basketball shot and soaring bird.

tips_and_updates

The Simple Truth

Think of a ball thrown forward. While it travels forward at a steady speed, gravity is pulling it down faster and faster. The "arc" you see is just these two independent movements happening at the exact same time.

sports_basketball Real-World Case

The Perfect Jump Shot

When a basketball player shoots, the ball is a projectile. The horizontal distance to the hoop must perfectly match the vertical arc created by gravity to ensure the ball drops through the net.
